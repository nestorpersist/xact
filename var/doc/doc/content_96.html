Syntax
<primary> ::= <name> | <int> | <float> | <string>
<primary> ::= '(' S? <exp> S? ')'
<primary> ::= '[' S? <exps> S? ']'

Description

Parenthesis are used to change the grouping of operators by precedence. Square brackets are sugar for a list constructor.

Semantics

A parenthesized expression is semantically equivalent to the expression it contains.

The expression [2,3] is equivalent to list(2,3).

Parsing

During parsing parenthesis are removed. After parsing the grouping of operators is encoded in the tree of xdom:call nodes. Parenthesis are reinserted, when necessary, during unparsing. Parsing followed by unparsing will remove any unnecessary parenthesis.

For the square bracket list constructor, the xdom:call node will have kind "list".
